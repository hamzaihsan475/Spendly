Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us. This includes your account information (name, email address, password) and expense data that you choose to log (amounts, categories, dates, descriptions, and notes). We also collect usage data to help us improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Specifically, we use your expense data to generate summaries, charts, and insights about your spending patterns. We use your contact information to communicate with you about the service, send updates, and respond to your inquiries. We do not use your personal financial data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption and security measures. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your expense records are backed up regularly to prevent data loss.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may use trusted third-party service providers to help us operate our business (such as hosting, analytics, or customer support), but these partners are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can export all your expense data from your account settings. If you choose to delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We are committed to resolving any concerns you may have about how we handle your information.
